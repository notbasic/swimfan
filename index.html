Lets get the basics

The truely first step to learning how to swim would be learning how to float. Once you have mastered this task you can move on to step as a begginger learning how to swim. Once you have master floating it will began to build up your confidence. fun fact: The human body composed of an averaging around 57-60% water.

more of the basics

Along with floating sinking is just as important when it comes to swimming. learning how to sink will give the swimmer more control and confidence when they do go under the water. A relaxed person in the water will naturally float. Some water games played requiring being able to sink.
